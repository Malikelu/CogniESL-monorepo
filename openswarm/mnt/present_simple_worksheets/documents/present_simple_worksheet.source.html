Present Simple Worksheet

Level: Adult Learners | L1: Portuguese | Age: Adults

Section A: Controlled Practice

1. Fill in the Blanks

I ___ (to go) to the gym every day.

She ___ (to read) books in the evening.

They ___ (to work) from 9 AM to 5 PM.

He ___ (to play) soccer on weekends.

2. Negative Transformation

Rewrite the following sentences in the negative form:

I eat breakfast at 7 AM.

She likes coffee.

They travel often.

He watches TV every night.

3. Question Formation

Formulate questions based on the statements:

They cook dinner together.

She plays the piano.

You work at a bank.

He studies English.

Section B: Short Paragraph on Daily Routines

Write a short paragraph about your daily routine using the present simple tense. Include at least four activities.

Answer Key

1. Fill in the Blanks

I go to the gym every day.

She reads books in the evening.

They work from 9 AM to 5 PM.

He plays soccer on weekends.

2. Negative Transformation

I do not eat breakfast at 7 AM.

She does not like coffee.

They do not travel often.

He does not watch TV every night.

3. Question Formation

Do they cook dinner together?

Does she play the piano?

Do you work at a bank?

Does he study English?
