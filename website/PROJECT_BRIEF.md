# CogniESL Website — Project Brief

## Product
CogniESL is an AI-powered web app that helps ESL teachers save time on lesson planning and material preparation. Its core differentiator is L1 interference intelligence — helping teachers understand WHY students from different language backgrounds make specific mistakes.

## Brand Architecture
- **Cognicrafted** = umbrella brand (future products)
- **CogniESL** = this product

## Domains
- cogniESL.com (product)
- cognicrafted.com (umbrella, later)

## Target Audience
- Primary: Individual ESL teachers in the US
- Secondary: Schools/companies (corporate plans, future)
- Tertiary: Students (self-study, future)

## Key Messaging
1. **Time savings** is the hook — teachers lose hours on material prep
2. **L1 intelligence** is the differentiator — no competitor combines this with material generation
3. **AI-comfortable** — ease resistant teachers into AI
4. **Built by a teacher** — relatable, not corporate
